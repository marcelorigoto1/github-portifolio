3D Mapping Project

General report of fieldwork, data processing, and 3D modeling activities.

Overview

The project combined drone-based and ground-based data acquisition with advanced 3D modeling techniques. Fieldwork was supported by topographic surveys and processed using photogrammetry and specialized software.

Main Activities

  • Data collection with drones and ground scanners.
  • Review of technical documentation and workflows.
  • Photogrammetry processing with Agisoft Metashape.
  • 3D modeling and analysis with 3DReshaper.
  • Data integration, processing, and cleaning.

Outcomes

Generated preliminary 3D models, structured workflows, and validated datasets, ready for the production of orthomosaics, digital elevation models, and textured 3D meshes.
