Digital Medium

The complete digital medium (source code, exported shapefiles, sample data, and compiled application) is attached to the submitted thesis as a physical storage medium per department requirements. The contents of the digital medium mirror the folder structure described below.

Digital Medium Contents

  • src/ — Full Unity project source (Assets/, ProjectSettings/, Packages/)
  • data/ — Sample shapefiles and CSV exports for each of the four study areas
  • docs/ — Thesis PDF, poster PDF, and appendices
  • build/ — Compiled Windows standalone application
  • web/ — This website (rigoto25/)
